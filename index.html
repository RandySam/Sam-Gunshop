Frequently Asked Questions

What are your shipping policies?
We ship nationwide following all federal and state regulations. All firearms must be shipped to a licensed FFL dealer. Shipping times are typically 3-5 business days. We use insured shipping for all orders.
Do you offer background check services?
Yes, we facilitate all required background checks through licensed FFL dealers. All sales comply with federal and state laws. Please bring valid ID and any required permits.
What is your return policy?
Returns are accepted within 30 days of purchase for unused items in original packaging. Firearms must be returned to the original FFL dealer. A 15% restocking fee applies to all returns.
Do you offer layaway services?
Yes, we offer layaway services for purchases over $500. A 20% deposit is required to hold items for up to 90 days. Items must be paid in full before pickup.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept cash, check, and major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express). All transactions are processed securely and in compliance with banking regulations.
